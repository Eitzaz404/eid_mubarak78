Eid Mubarak

FROM ABRAR

May this blessed occasion bring you and your family peace, joy, and countless blessings.

🌙 Special Duas 🌙

تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ

"Taqabbalallahu minna wa minkum"

May Allah accept from us and from you

عِيدُكُمْ مُبَارَكٌ

"Eidukum Mubarak"

May your Eid be blessed

جَعَلَكُمُ اللَّهُ مِنَ الْعَائِدِينَ

"Ja'alakumullahu minal 'a'idin"

May Allah make you among those who return (to righteousness)

📖 About Eid-ul-Fitr

Eid-ul-Fitr is a blessed festival that marks the end of Ramadan, the holy month of fasting.

It is a day of joy, gratitude, and celebration with family and friends.

Muslims around the world gather for special prayers, share meals, and give charity (Zakat al-Fitr) to those in need.

✨ Traditions ✨

  • 🕌 Special Eid prayer (Salat al-Eid)
  • 🍽️ Festive meals with family
  • 🎁 Giving gifts (Eidi) to children
  • 🤝 Visiting relatives and friends
